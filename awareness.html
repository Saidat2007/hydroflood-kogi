Community Safety Strategy

HydroFlood Kogi is committed to community resilience. By reporting hazards—ranging from blocked drainage to signs of river overflow—you enable us to take proactive measures to protect our environment and homes.

How You Can Protect Your Neighborhood

  • Stop Drainage Dumping: Never dispose of household waste into gutters, streams, or street drains.
  • Respect Collection Points: Always use designated central collection points.
  • Pre-Rainy Season Cleanup: Participate in local sanitation days to clear blocked drains around your property.
  • Waste Sorting: Keep plastic bottles and metals aside; they often have recycling value.
